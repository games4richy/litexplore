Information We Collect

When you contact us via our contact form or email, we collect the information you choose to provide, including your name and email address. We use this solely to respond to your enquiry and do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Doing so may affect some functionality of the Site.
