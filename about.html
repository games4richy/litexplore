About LitExplore

Honest travel guides, no sponsored rankings, no inflated reviews. Just real advice from people who have been there.

Who We Are

LitExplore is a travel publishing site dedicated to one thing: giving travelers accurate, useful information they can actually act on. We publish in-depth destination guides, practical how-to articles on travel logistics, visa guides written for real applicants rather than hypothetical visitors, and honest reviews of hotels, booking platforms, and travel services.

We started LitExplore because most travel content online is written around affiliate commissions rather than reader value. Sponsored rankings. Paid placements dressed as editorial recommendations. Generic lists recycled from other generic lists. We built something different: guides researched from the ground up, written in a first-person voice that acknowledges both what is great about a destination and what is difficult, frustrating, or overrated.

What We Believe

Honest over optimistic. If a visa application process is difficult or has a high rejection rate for certain passport holders, we say so clearly rather than glossing over it. If a destination has a known problem, we include it. Our readers are adults making real decisions with real money, and they deserve accurate information rather than perpetually sunny travel writing.

Specific over vague. We give actual prices, actual processing times, actual daily budgets, actual distances and transit times. Advice like "accommodation is affordable" or "the food scene is excellent" is useless to someone planning a trip. We say what a hostel dorm costs in baht, what a Schengen visa processing window is in weeks, and what the BTS ride from On Nut to Siam costs in dollars.

Relevant to a global audience. Most travel content is written from a US or UK perspective and assumes travel experiences that look nothing like what travelers from Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, or other African countries actually encounter. Visa requirements, flight routing, currency considerations, and passport strength all look different depending on where you hold citizenship. We write for that reality.

How We Make Money

LitExplore earns revenue through display advertising. We do not accept sponsored content, paid placements, or affiliate payments that influence editorial decisions. No company can pay to appear in our guides or influence how we rank, review, or describe a service. When we recommend a booking platform, a visa service, or a travel tool, it is because we think it is genuinely useful, not because we have a financial relationship with the company.

We participate in some affiliate programs (for example, we may earn a small commission when a reader books through a link we include in an article). When this applies, the affiliate relationship does not influence our editorial assessment of the product or service. We would recommend it regardless of whether a commission is available.
